## 2025-05-15 - [Single-pass Chunk Writing] **Learning:** Non-cached chunks like `(ChunkType, &[u8])` previously required two passes over the data (one for CRC32 calculation via the `Chunk` trait and one for writing). This created significant overhead in hot paths like `ChunkStreamWriter`. **Action:** Implement `CrcWriter` and `ChunkWriter::write_chunk_single_pass` to calculate CRC32 on-the-fly during write, reducing uncompressed/unencrypted write time by ~19%.
